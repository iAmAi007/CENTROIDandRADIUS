This tool calculates the geographical center (centroid) of a given set of coordinates. You can input up to 10 points and visualize the area on a map.

Accepted Input Formats: Decimal degrees e.g 37.7749, -122.4194; directional decimal degrees e.g 37.7749N, 122.4194W; degrees and minutes with direction e.g 37°46'N, 122°25'W; degrees and minutes without symbols e.g 04 45S, 040 30E & degrees, minutes and seconds without symbols e.g 04 45 00S, 040 30 00E.

Ensure the input format is consistent and valid. The latitude must range from -90 to 90, and the longitude must range from -180 to 180.
